Gym Exercises

Chest - Bench Press

A classic exercise to build chest muscles using a barbell or dumbbells.

Back - Deadlift

A full-body compound exercise primarily targeting the back and hamstrings.

Legs - Squats

Essential for building lower body strength, especially quads and glutes.

Shoulders - Overhead Press

Improves shoulder and tricep strength using dumbbells or barbell.

Biceps - Dumbbell Curl

Isolates the biceps for maximum muscle engagement.

Triceps - Tricep Dips

Targets triceps using parallel bars or a bench.

Core - Planks

Strengthens the core and improves stability and posture.
